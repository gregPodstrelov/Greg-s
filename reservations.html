Important for a real restaurant

This demo stores reservations in the browser that submitted them. That means it works for testing, but it is not enough for a real public website.

For a real restaurant, connect the form to a service like Formspree, Airtable, Supabase, Firebase, Google Sheets, OpenTable, Resy, or Tock. Then reservations will be visible from any device.
